What I Care About

Traveling exposes me to new environments and cultures. It helps me become more open-minded and observant.

Learning

I enjoy learning new skills, especially in technology. It keeps me growing and helps me improve step by step.

Baking teaches patience, accuracy, and following steps properly. It also helps me stay focused on process and details.

Art

Art helps me understand creativity, balance, and visual thinking. It improves how I look at design and layout.

Going Out

Going out helps me relax and clear my mind. It gives me new ideas and keeps my thinking fresh.
